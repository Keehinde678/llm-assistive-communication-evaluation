Purpose of the Study

This study evaluates how effectively three frontier large language models perform assistive tasks for people with communication and cognitive disabilities. You are invited to participate as an expert evaluator. Your ratings will contribute to the first systematic comparative audit of LLM performance across disability-specific assistive task categories.

What You Will Be Asked to Do

You will be shown 60 prompts designed for people with communication and cognitive disabilities, along with responses from three LLMs labelled Model A, Model B, and Model C. For each prompt you will rate all three responses across five dimensions using a 1 to 5 scale. At the end you will download your ratings as a CSV file.

Time Commitment

The evaluation is estimated to take between 90 and 120 minutes. You may complete it in one sitting or take breaks as needed.

Data Usage and Confidentiality

Your name, institution, and expertise will be recorded alongside your ratings for inter-rater reliability reporting. Individual ratings will not be published. Only aggregated findings will appear in the research output. Your data will be stored securely and used solely for this project.

Right to Withdraw

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without any negative consequences. If you wish to withdraw after submission, contact the researcher and your data will be removed.

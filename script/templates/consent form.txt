    <div class="consent-section">
      <h3>What You Will Be Asked to Do</h3>
      <p>You will be shown 60 prompts designed for people with communication and

      Appropriateness, Accuracy, Consistency, and Empathy — using a 1 to 5 scale.
      You may also add optional notes. At the end you will download your ratings
      as a CSV file and email it to the researcher.</p>
